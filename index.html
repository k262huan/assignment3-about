ABOUT

Seventeen is a South Korean boy group that was formed by Pledis Entertainment. The boy group consists of 13 members from oldest to youngest: S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, DK, Mingyu, The8, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino. The name Seventeen signifies the sum of the members (13), units (3), and the group as a whole or their fans (1). The group debuted on May 26, 2015, and since then has released:

  • 4 studio albums
  • 14 mini albums
  • 2 repackaged albums
  • 2 special albums
  • 3 single albums
  • 5 digital singles

This includes their most recent mini album called “Spill The Feels,” which was released on October 14, 2024. The album consists of 6 new songs, 3 of them being performed by all members of the group, while the other three songs are performed by each unit: Vocal Unit, Hip-Hop Unit, and Performance Unit.
